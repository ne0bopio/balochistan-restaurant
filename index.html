Our Story

From the Heart of Balochistan

✦

Balochistan — Pakistan's vast, ancient western province — has a culinary heritage as rich as its landscape. Its food is simple, bold, and deeply rooted in nomadic tradition: whole animals slow-roasted over open fire, spiced with nothing more than salt, pepper, and time.

We brought those traditions to Bergen County, New Jersey in 2025 — and the response has been extraordinary. Balochistan Restaurant is the first and only authentic Balochi Pakistani restaurant in the region, and every dish we serve is a tribute to the people, culture, and flavors of that remarkable land.

🕌
100% Certified Halal Every ingredient, every dish — prepared to the highest halal standards, always.
🔥
Traditional Open-Fire Cooking Our signature sajji is slow-roasted the Baloch way — whole, on the bone, over live fire.
🌿
Fresh, Quality Ingredients We source the finest meats and spices so every bite carries authentic, honest flavor.

What We Serve

Authentic Flavors of Pakistan

Every dish tells a story. From smoky mountain kebabs to slow-simmered karahis, our menu celebrates the full depth of Pakistani cuisine with a proud Baloch soul.

✦ Signature Dish

Lamb Sajji

The crown jewel of Balochi cuisine. A whole lamb marinated in a secret blend of spices and slow-roasted over an open fire for hours until the meat is impossibly tender and the skin crackles with smoky perfection. Served with aromatic Sajji rice — this is the dish that defines us.

Available whole or by portion. Advance order recommended for whole lamb.

Karahi

Mutton Karahi

Bone-in mutton slow-cooked in a steel wok with tomatoes, green chilies, ginger, garlic, and a fierce blend of spices. Rich, oily in the best way, and deeply fragrant — served with naan straight from the tandoor.

✓ Halal
Rice Dish

Biryani

Long-grain basmati rice layered with marinated meat, whole spices, fried onions, and saffron — sealed and slow-cooked (dum) to perfection. Every spoonful is a fragrant, spiced revelation.

✓ Halal
Grilled

Seekh Kebab

Minced lamb and beef mixed with herbs, onion, and traditional spices, hand-packed onto skewers and grilled over charcoal. Served with raita and fresh-baked naan.

✓ Halal
Grilled

Chicken Tikka

Bone-in chicken marinated overnight in yogurt, lemon, and bold spices, then grilled in our tandoor. Juicy, charred at the edges, and bursting with flavor.

✓ Halal
Slow Cooked

Nihari

A deeply spiced slow-cooked beef shank stew, simmered overnight until the meat melts from the bone. Garnished with ginger, lemon, and fried onions. The ultimate comfort dish.

✓ Halal
Bread

Fresh Tandoori Naan

Handmade and slapped onto the walls of our clay tandoor until perfectly blistered and chewy. Plain, garlic, or stuffed — the perfect companion to every dish.

✓ Halal
Balochi Special

Lamb Chops (Chaamp)

Thick-cut lamb rib chops marinated in yogurt and spices, then grilled over high heat for a smoky char. Tender, rich, and distinctly Baloch in character.

✓ Halal
Drink

Kashmiri Pink Chai

The iconic Pakistani pink tea — brewed from special gunpowder tea with milk and baking soda, topped with crushed pistachios and cardamom. Warm, comforting, and utterly unique.

✓ Halal
Dessert

Kheer & Gulab Jamun

Classic Pakistani desserts — velvety rice pudding scented with rose water and cardamom, alongside golden milk-solid dumplings soaked in sugar syrup. Sweet endings, done right.

✓ Halal
